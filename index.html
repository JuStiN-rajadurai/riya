About Uswhy choose us? Because we're the best.

Riya Enterprises is a premier manufacturer of highquality leather, proudly holding the prestigious Leather Working Group (LWG) Gold Certification. Established in 2018, we specialize in sustainable, ethically sourced, and technically advanced leather solutions for the fashion, footwear, and Premium Leather Garments industries worldwide. With a monthly production capacity of 500,000 sq. ft., our focus remains on delivering consistent quality, innovation, and eco-conscious practices that align with global standards.

Wet Blue Selection

Careful selection of wet blue hides to ensure quality and consistency.

Neutralization

Balancing the pH of the leather to prepare it for further processing.

Re-tanning

Enhancing the leather’s properties such as texture, flexibility, and durability

Dyeing

Adding color to the leather to achieve the desired aesthetic.

Fat Liquoring

Applying oils and fats to soften and lubricate the leather fibers.

Drying

Reducing the moisture content of the leather to the optimal level.

Conditioning

Further softening and preparing the leather for finishing.

Finishing

Applying surface treatments to protect and enhance the leather’s appearance.

Quality Control

Final inspection to ensure the leather meets our high standards.
