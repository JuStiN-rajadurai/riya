About Us

Manufactures and Tanners of Finished Leather

Riya Enterprises is a premier manufacturer of high-quality leather, proudly holding the prestigious Leather Working Group (LWG) Gold Certification. Established in 2018, we specialize in sustainable, ethically sourced, and technically advanced leather solutions for the fashion, footwear, and Premium Leather Garments industries worldwide.

With a monthly production capacity of 500,000 sq. ft., our focus remains on delivering consistent quality, innovation, and eco-conscious practices that align with global standards.
